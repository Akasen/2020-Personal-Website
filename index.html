Previous Projects

Senior Project - CPAT

My Senior Project in University was a two semester long course where me and three other students assisted a local member of the Florida community with a non-mission crital aspect of a project they wanted us to work on.

The project, the Course Planning and Advising Tool, was to provide a more meaningful plan so that students could better assess their education. An issue had by many schools is with the demand for students to graduate on average, that is to say within four years for a Bachelors Degree. It is understandable that in some cases, a student simply doesn't have the means to properly plan in a concise way their track, even with the assistance of guidance counselors.

Thus, the initial goal of the project was to provide a proof of concept for a website that could allow for counselors to more easily track the progress of student, and for students to better plan their courses in the future, especially if they need to drop a course.

Admittedly though, this project was itself in my eyes far more of a learning experience in the many realities of software development. The amount of research we were doing, the amount of pre-planning, lowering of our own expectations and goals, and even having to confront our own knowlege and skill. On top of all that, having to as best as we can learn and apply concepts like AGILE deveopment and just the basics of web devlopment.

However, despite my own experiences in this project, I still found it thrilling to learn and solve problems. In a way, my wish to develop and create software was only strenthened in this project.
